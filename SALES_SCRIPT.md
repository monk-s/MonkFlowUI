# MonkFlow Sprint — Discovery Call Script

**Goal:** End the call with either (a) QuickBooks invoice link sent + deposit collected, OR (b) clear next step with a deadline.
**Length:** 30 minutes. Do not run over.
**Mindset:** You're a doctor. They're describing symptoms. Diagnose, prescribe, close. Do not pitch in the first 15 min.

---

## Pre-call (5 min before)
- Open their website, LinkedIn, Google reviews
- Note: # employees, services, obvious manual work signals (intake forms, "call us to book", spreadsheet downloads)
- Have QuickBooks invoice link, Calendly kickoff link, and proposal template open in tabs

---

## 0:00–0:02 — Open
> "Hey [name], thanks for jumping on. Quick agenda: I'm gonna ask you a bunch of questions about how your business runs day-to-day for about 15 minutes, then if it makes sense I'll walk you through what working together looks like. Sound good?"

Wait for yes. Don't fill silence.

---

## 0:02–0:07 — Their business (5 min)
- "Tell me what [company] actually does in one sentence."
- "How many people on the team?"
- "Who's drowning the most right now — you, ops, sales, fulfillment?"
- "What did your week look like last week — what ate the most time?"

**Listen for:** "I", "manually", "every time", "we have to", "spreadsheet", "copy-paste", "back and forth". Each one = a workflow you can quote.

---

## 0:07–0:17 — The dig (10 min) ⭐ MOST IMPORTANT SECTION
Pick the 3 biggest pain points they mentioned. For each, ask:

1. **"Walk me through how that works today, step by step."** (Make them describe the actual clicks.)
2. **"Who does it?"** (Person matters — owner time = expensive)
3. **"How long does each one take?"**
4. **"How often?"** (Per day, per client, per week?)
5. **"What happens if it doesn't get done?"** (Surfaces real cost)
6. **"What have you tried to fix it?"** (Reveals budget + sophistication)

**Math out loud:** "Okay so that's [X] hrs/week × $[Y]/hr × 52 weeks = $[Z] a year. That sound right?"

This is where the sale is made. They start selling themselves.

---

## 0:17–0:20 — Frame the sprint (3 min)
> "Okay so based on what you just told me, I think the 3 highest-leverage things to automate are: [workflow 1], [workflow 2], [workflow 3]. If those got handled automatically, you'd get back roughly [X hrs/week] — does that match how you'd prioritize?"

Wait. Let them confirm or re-rank.

> "Cool. Here's how I work. It's called the Sprint — $2,000 flat fee, 7 calendar days from kickoff to handoff. I build all 3, connect them to whatever you already use, you get a recorded training, and I include 30 days of free tweaks. $1,000 to start, $1,000 when I deliver. If I miss the deadline, you don't owe the second half."

---

## 0:20–0:25 — Close (5 min)
> "Two questions. One — does $2,000 to get [X hrs/week] back forever feel fair? Two — is there any reason we couldn't kick this off this week?"

**Possible answers:**

| Response | What you say |
|---|---|
| "Yes, let's do it" | "Awesome. I'm sending the QuickBooks invoice to your email right now — $1,000 deposit, payable by card or ACH. Once it clears I'll send the kickoff calendar invite for tomorrow." (CREATE IT IN QBO IMMEDIATELY — don't wait until after the call.) |
| "I need to think about it" | "Totally fair. What specifically are you weighing?" → Answer the real objection. Then: "I have 3 spots left in the April cohort. Can I hold one for you until [Friday]? If you're not in by then I'll release it." |
| "I need to talk to my partner" | "Smart. When are you talking to them — today or tomorrow? I'll follow up [day after] at [time]. Sound good?" Lock the time. |
| "Send me something to review" | "Of course. I'll have a 1-page proposal in your inbox in 2 hours. When can you review and respond — tomorrow morning work?" Lock the time. |
| "Too expensive" | "Got it. Quick math — [X hrs/week] × $[Y]/hr is $[Z]/week, so this pays for itself in [N] weeks. After that it's pure profit forever. Is the issue cash flow this month, or do you not believe the time savings are real?" Then handle the actual objection. |
| "Can you do it for less?" | "I have a smaller version — 1 automation, $750, 3-day turnaround. Want me to scope that instead?" |

---

## 0:25–0:30 — Lock the next step
NEVER end without a specific next action and a specific time.

- Deposit collected? → "Kickoff call tomorrow at [time]. I'll send the invite now."
- Proposal needed? → "I'll send it in 2 hours. We talk again [day] at [time]."
- Thinking? → "I'll follow up [day] at [time]. If I don't hear back I'll release the spot."

Always end with: **"Anything else I should know before we wrap?"** (Surfaces hidden objections.)

---

## After the call (immediate, before next call)
1. Log to PIPELINE.md: name, company, stage, $ value, next action, next-action date
2. Send QuickBooks invoice link / proposal / calendar invite — within 5 minutes
3. LinkedIn connect with personalized note: "Great call today, [name]. [One specific thing they said]."

---

## Objection bank (memorize)

- **"We're not ready for automation yet"** → "Most of my clients said that. Ready means you're already drowning. The 3 things I'd build for you would save [X hrs] starting day 8. What does 'ready' look like to you?"
- **"We tried Zapier/Make and it didn't work"** → "Yeah, those are great for tinkerers but they break and nobody owns them. The difference is I build it, I own it for 30 days, and it's hosted in one place — not 14 disconnected zaps."
- **"How do I know you can deliver?"** → "Two things. One — money-back if I miss the deadline. Two — I'll show you a live build in MonkFlow right now if you want." (Then SHARE SCREEN.)
- **"We're a small business, this isn't for us"** → "5–50 person businesses are exactly who this is for. Big companies have IT teams. You're the one losing your weekends — that's literally why I built this."
- **"Send me references"** → "I'm building the April cohort right now and I'm being upfront about that. Reference clients come from this cohort. The deal: half-price ($2k vs $3k after May), and you become the case study. Fair trade?"

## Daily targets
- **Calls booked:** 4–6/day
- **Show rate:** ≥70% (confirm 24h before, 1h before)
- **Close rate target:** 25% on call (1–2 deposits/day)
- **Proposal-to-close after call:** 50% within 48 hrs
